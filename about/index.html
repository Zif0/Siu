About Us

Welcome to Siu, your premier destination for authentic Korean BBQ. Our restaurant is dedicated to providing you with an unforgettable dining experience, where you can enjoy the rich flavors and vibrant culture of Korea.

At Siu, we pride ourselves on using only the freshest ingredients and premium meats to bring you the best Korean BBQ in town. Whether you're a seasoned fan of Korean cuisine or trying it for the first time, our menu has something for everyone.

Our History

Siu was founded with the vision of bringing authentic Korean BBQ to our community. Since our opening, we have been dedicated to providing high-quality food and exceptional service.

The chef has nearly 30 years of culinary experience and has dedicated his work to specialize in Korean food. He takes pride in providing an authentic and honest experience.
